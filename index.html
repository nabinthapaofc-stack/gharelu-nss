About Gharelu

What is Gharelu?

Gharelu is a web-based platform designed to simplify how households access everyday services. Whether you need a plumber, electrician, or cleaning professional, Gharelu connects you with verified local providers — quickly and effortlessly. No phone calls, no middlemen.
